ROTARY

From humble beginnings in a small workshop in the Swiss town of La Chaux-de-Fonds to achieving super brand status, Rotary Watches possesses a unique and vibrant history over the course of its 124 years.
In 1905, Rotary relocated to London and has since maintained its worldwide reputation as an icon of British design and watch making excellence, striving always to deliver unparalleled quality timepieces at an exceptional price.
Rotary's extensive watch collections include classic timepieces inspired by their dynamic and wide-ranging history.
Whether you choose an exquisite skeleton dial watch or a sophisticated timepiece with a sportier aesthetic, you can be confident in knowing you own a timepiece of unwavering quality and unrivalled precision with a Rotary Watch.
