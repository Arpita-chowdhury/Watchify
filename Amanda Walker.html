Amanda Walker

A collection of timeless, elegant and understated watches for empowered women. After 25 years curating and designing collections for several iconic British and International brands Amanda Walker has just launched her first collection. Thoroughly feminine timepieces that champion simplicity, style and sophistication. “There’s no present like time” the strap line, forms the ethos behind the brand in reminding us each day to value the importance of time well spent.
Amanda Walker watches channel quiet grace, a discerning eye and an utterly exquisite gifting experience.
