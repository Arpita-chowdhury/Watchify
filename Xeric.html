Xeric

Xeric officially launched in November 2013 with our first Kickstarter campaign for the Xeriscope, but our origin story really begins in 1998 when Mitch Greenblatt received his first watch (ever).
He began collecting and blogging about unusual vintage watches and became an influential watch blogger and authority on the subject of modern horology.
  After years of offering the most unique timepieces from around the world on Watchismo, Mitch & Andrew hired Danny Hunsaker in 2013 and together we created Xeric.
We've since launched 11 successful Kickstarter campaigns including the most funded timepiece in Kickstarter history. We deconstruct the traditions of watch designs and push the boundaries of how to display time on a mechanical watch.
